The science

Your body produces over 50 hormones — and estrogen affects nearly all of them.

When estrogen declines, it doesn’t just cause hot flashes. It affects your brain, bones, skin, hair, metabolism, mood, sleep, and cardiovascular system. That’s why symptoms feel like they’re everywhere — because hormonally, they are.
