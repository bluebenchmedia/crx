Our Treatment Formula

ClearedRx treatments are prescription hormone therapy. Your formula is tailored to you and made under strict pharmacy rules.

What's in the formula

Our treatments contain bioidentical estradiol (the primary estrogen your body loses during menopause) and, where appropriate, progesterone to protect the uterine lining. Compounded creams also include estriol, an additional estrogen that supports vaginal tissue, skin hydration, and comfort.

Made in a US compounding pharmacy

Every order is prepared in a licensed US compounding pharmacy. Your medication is compounded fresh for your order — not bottled months ago and left on a shelf.

Quality and feel

Our creams are non-comedogenic (won't clog pores), lightweight, and non-greasy for easy daily use. Everything is medical grade and meant for prescription use.

How It Works

Hormone replacement therapy replaces the estrogen and progesterone your body loses during menopause. It's an ongoing treatment — you use it consistently so your hormone levels stay balanced.

Creams & gels

Applied once daily to clean, dry skin — your arm, shoulder, or as directed. A measured dispenser or pump delivers your exact dose every time. Dries quickly, no residue, nothing to swallow.

Estrogen patches

A small, discreet patch worn on your lower abdomen, buttock, or upper thigh. Change it twice a week and it works around the clock — no daily routine to remember.

Pills & tablets

One small pill taken once daily as prescribed — the simplest routine possible. Estrogen pills are typically taken in the morning; if progesterone is included, it's taken at bedtime for better sleep.
